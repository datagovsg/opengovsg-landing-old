UX Designer

You bridge user needs and business goals. You make complex systems easy to use. You make the product look good and work well.

John Smith joined us in 2016 as a designer and this is what he has to say about working here.

What does a UX Designer do?

  • Communicate with users and other stakeholders to understand their need.
  • Conduct user research and testing on designs.
  • Make interative prototypes and wireframes to visualise concepts and screens flow.
  • Design user interfaces and define visual styles for products.
  • Work with Project managers and Software Engineers to implement designs.

Who are we looking for?

  • Degree or Diploma in Human Computer Interactions, Design, Psychology, Computer Sciences or related disciplines is preferred.
  • A portfolio that demonstrates your ability to create great user experience.
  • Great visual design sense and an eye for detail.
  • Has a passion for making products for public good.
  • Able to translate and simplify complex systems into visual concepts.
  • Able to communicate and work in teams.
  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite and other prototyping softwares such as Sketch and Invision.
  • Experience in Agile and UX development environment will be an added advantage.
  • Familiar with HTML, CSS and JavaScript and other web development technologies will be an added advantage.
  • Candidates with more experience will be considered for senior position.
