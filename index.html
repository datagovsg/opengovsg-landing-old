Some things we've made

Beeline.sg

Beeline is a data-driven shuttle bus platform that empowers commuters to co-create routes to meet their travel needs. Bus routes are created from user suggestions and public transport usage data, and users book and pay for rides using a mobile app. Beeline went live on Aug 2015. To date, 250+ routes are live, with 8,000+ users having travelled on the platform.

Github Website

Data.gov.sg

Data.gov.sg is a one-stop portal for all publicly available government dataset. The site goes beyond being a data repository, and aims to make government data relevant and understandable to the public, through the active use of charts and articles. There are more than 1,200 datasets available, with around 900,000 users visiting the site in 2017.

Website

Parking.sg

Parking.SG is a mobile app alternative to parking coupons. Data collected from the app may open up possible evidence-based car park planning and management policy. It went live in Oct 2017 and has been downloaded more than 380,000 times.
