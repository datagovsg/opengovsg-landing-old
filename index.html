Some things we've made

Data.gov.sg

A open repository of all the Singapore Government's public data. It helps people understand the data using visualizations and articles, and provides realtime APIs for developers to use.

Website

Beeline.sg

Beeline is a data-driven shuttle bus platform. Bus routes are created from user suggestions and public transport usage data. Users can book, pay for, and track rides using a mobile app.

Website

Parking.sg

Parking.sg is a mobile app alternative to parking coupons. It lets users pay, extend, and refund their parking sessions just using their phones.

Website

Forms.sg

FormSG is a form builder tool for agencies to self-service and create online forms that capture classified data, with the goal of replacing paper forms.

Our Work Culture

Sense of Ownership

See a problem in the government? Stop complaining, prototype and propose a solution. Brainstorm it together at in between projects or during hackathons.

Continuous Learning

Share your knowledge and learn from each other at our regular learning lunches. Allocate time for self-improvement. Go for conferences and workshops. Learning never stops.

Flexible Work Arrangements

Plan your schedule. Work from home if you need to. As long was you deliver and works well with your team, you don't need be physically in office all the time.

Serious and Fun Colleagues

We support each other. We believe in hiring the right people, and treating them well. We do serious work and chill over beers, BBQ and hotpots.
