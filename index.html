What We Do

Design

We identify areas in the public space where technology can help and build prototypes to show it.

Evangelise

We present prototypes to stakeholders and other government agencies and persuade them to test the idea.

Implement

We pilot ideas and build them out if the tested prototypes are beneficial for the citizens.

How We Do It

Solve real problems.

Work on meaningful projects from transport to healthcare to how public servants collaborate and how citizens interact with the government. Don’t waste your skills building another shopping cart.

Build for the users.

Develop products for public good. Not for download numbers, not for KPI, not for our Bosses, Ministers, Perm Sects, Directors, CEO etc.

Push for change.

Government IT systems need not take years to build. They need not be million dollars ITQ. Challenge the status quo one project at a time. We start small, do it well and iterate continuously.

Leverage on open.

Cloud services and open source tools can be utilised to build stable and secure systems. We also aim to open source our repos and provide data as much as possible so that the community can co-create with us.

We are serious and fun and always learning.

We are public servants but we wear t-shirts and jeans on most days when we are not attending meetings.
